### A. The "Mini" Paradox
Contradicting the "Scaling Laws," the smaller model (**GPT-5-mini**) demonstrated superior information density and stability compared to the larger model. It maintains a Fixed Point 36% higher than GPT-5 Standard, making it the ideal candidate for short-term working memory in autonomous agents.

The model identifies recursive prompts as "repetitive/unsafe" and collapses to a null output (`\n\n`). This confirms Opus 4.6 is suitable only for supervised, linear tasks, not autonomous loops.
